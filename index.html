Requirements and Rules

Read the following requirements and rules to participate in our leagues and tournaments.

  • 1. All players must be registered and verified.
  • 2. Players must adhere to the code of conduct during events.
  • 3. Team managers are responsible for submitting accurate player statistics.
  • 4. Cheating or unsportsmanlike behavior will result in disqualification.
  • 5. Matches must be played at the scheduled times unless otherwise agreed.
  • 6. Players must use their official gamer tags during matches.
  • 7. Any disputes must be reported within 24 hours of the match.
  • 8. Respect all players, officials, and the community.
  • 9. Follow the official game rules and guidelines.
  • 10. Ensure proper communication and coordination within your team.
